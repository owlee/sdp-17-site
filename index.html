Emota Technology

Empowers socially impaired individuals in reading the emotions of their conversation partner.

Emota accurately translates facial expression of the that person through Emota servers.

Emota notifies you of the reading, and sends to emergency contacts in sticky situations.

How it Works

Unobtrusive Body

A small tiny body involving a micro-camera and a rechargeable board body.

Responsive Server

Facial information is parsed and interchanged seamlessly between Cloud services and board.

SMS powered

Reliable SMS services send to the user. This allows dedicated services dependent on cellular uptime. In case of unwanted interactions, Emota will alert all emergency contacts.
